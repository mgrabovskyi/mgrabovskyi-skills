# In progress

Drafts that aren't ready to ship. Skills here are intentionally **not** listed in the top-level `README.md` and are excluded from the published skill set.

Promote a skill out of this bucket once:

1. The `description` frontmatter is trigger-rich (names concrete situations or phrases that should invoke it, not just topics).
2. The body is focused and short enough to read top-to-bottom.
3. It has been used at least once on a real task — not theoretical.
4. It's been added to its destination bucket's `README.md` and to the root `README.md`.
